Cheesecake

Don't run away! This one isn't so bad. This cheesecake is lactose free. No, it's not vegan - I doubt there's a way to make vegan cheesecake, but at least you won't fall sick from the lactose.

Ingredients

  • 4 block of lactose free cheese
  • 1.5 cups of crushed graham cracker
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 0.5 cup sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • Raspberry jam

Steps

  1. Leave eggs and cheese on the counter to come to room temperature
  2. Crush the graham cracker if you haven't already done so. Then mix in the melted butter.
  3. In a springform pan lined with parchment paper, pour the graham cracker and press down to form a solid base
  4. Bake at 350 F for 10 minutes. Then remove from oven and let cool. Leave the oven on and reduce heat to 325 F
  5. While the base is cooling, prepare the cheese filling. In a mixing bowl, start mixing the cheese on low speed.
  6. Add the eggs one at a time, then add the sugar and vanilla and mix on medium high until smooth
  7. Pour the cheese filling into the spring form pan and gently tap it down on the counter so it spreads evenly
  8. Bake for 40 minutes on the middle rack.
  9. Once baking is complete, turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake in the oven to cool slowly, leaving the oven door open a crack.
  10. After 30 minutes, open the oven door wider and let the cake continue to cool for another 30 minutes. This will keep it from cracking.
  11. Remove the cake from the oven and let sit on the counter until slightly warm, then transfer to the refrigerator to chill.
  12. Once chilled, top with raspberry jam and serve!

The End!
